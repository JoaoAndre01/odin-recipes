Risotto

Description

The traditional recipe from Italy.

cooked the slow and painful way, but oh so worth it. Complements grilled meats and chicken dishes very well.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Broth 6
  • Olive Oil 3 tablespoons
  • Portobello Mushroom 1 pound
  • White Mushroom 1 pound/li>
  • Shallots 2, diced
  • Arborio Rice 1 1/2 cups
  • Chives 3 tablespoons
  • Oregano
  • Butter 4 tablespoons
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese 1/3 cup

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, warm the broth over low heat
  2. Warm the olive oil over medium-heat and stir in the mushrooms for 3 minutes
  3. Remove mushrooms and their liquid
  4. Add olive oil to skillet, and stir in the shallots
  5. Cook 1 minute and add rice, stirring to coat with oil, about 2 minutes
  6. Pour in the wine, stirring until fully absorbed
  7. Add 1/2 cup broth to the rice and stir until absorbed
  8. Continue adding 1/2 cup of broth until the liquid is absorbed and the rice is al dente
  9. Remove from heat and stir in the mushrroms with their liquid, butter, chives and parmesan
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!
