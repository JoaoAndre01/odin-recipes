Lasagna

Description

The traditional recipe from Italy.

It takes a little work... but it's definitely worth it!

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian Sausage 1 pound
  • Lean Ground Beef 3/4 pound
  • Garlic 2 cloves, crushed
  • Onion 1/2 cup
  • Can Crushed Tomatoes 1 (28 ounces)
  • Can Tomato Paste 2 (6 ounces)
  • White Sugar 2 tablespoons
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Basil Leaves
  • Olive Oil
  • Lasagna Noodles 12
  • Ricotta Cheese 16 ounces
  • Egg 1
  • Mozzarella Cheese 3/4 pound
  • Parmesan Cheese 3/4 cup
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. In a Dutch over, cook sausage, beef, onion and garlic over medium heat
  2. Stir crushed tomatoes, paste, sauce and water
  3. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, salt and pepper
  4. Simmer, covered, for about 30 minutes
  5. Cook the lasagna noodles in boiling water for 10 minutes
  6. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water
  7. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg and 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. Preheat over to 375ยบ F (190ยบ C)
  9. Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a dish and arrange 6 noodles over meat sauce
  10. Top with 1/3 of mozzarella cheese slices and spoon 1 1/2 cup meat sauce over mozzarella
  11. Cover with foil and bake oven for 25 minutes
  12. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes
  13. Cool for 15 minutes before serving and Enjoy!
