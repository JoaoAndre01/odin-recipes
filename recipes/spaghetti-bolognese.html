Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

The traditional recipe from Italy.

An easy family favourite recipe for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti 400g
  • Pork 100g
  • Carrot 1
  • Onion 1
  • White Wine 100ml
  • Tomato Sauce 200g
  • Milk 50ml
  • Oregano
  • Basil Leaves
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the onion and carrot, then chope them finely
  2. Pour some oil in the pot and set it over medium heat
  3. Add the onion and carrot with salt when the oil is hot
  4. Cook them for about 10 minutes
  5. Add the meat and mix it so it doesn't stick together in big pieces
  6. Add salt, pepper and oregano to taste
  7. When the meat is no longer pink, increase heat to high and add the white wine
  8. After 2-3 minutes lower the heat and add the tomato sauce and milk. Let it simmer for 20 minutes
  9. Put abundant water to boil and add the spaghetti with a teaspoon of salt
  10. When the spaghetti is ready, add it to the Bolognese sauce put with the fire off and mix well
  11. Serve with a few basil leaves. Enjoy!
